Auto Remind

Automatically ping you at a specified time before the pound opens.

Image

Displays pet image and rarity from the given link.

Oekaki

Displays Oekaki drawing and stats from the given link.

Pet

Displays pet image as well as pet information from the given link.

Time

Displays the time before the pound opens or if it's already open.

Remindme

Legacy form of Auto Remind, where you set a time and will ping you when the time is up.
